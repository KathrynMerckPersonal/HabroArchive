HABROPEDIA

An unofficial archive of HABROMANIA lore―primary sources and some fan theories about the world's lore!

❝HABROMANIA is a dreamy (or nightmarish) Alice in Wonderland-inspired RPG that follows a 19-year-old Alice’s quest to escape Wonderland- with or without her sanity intact.

"HABROMANIA is a point-and-click, choice-based adventure game with branching endings and undertones of psychological horror.

"Alice's journey is fraught with choices that determine whether she escapes Wonderland and who she hurts or helps along the way. It's been compared to other beloved indie games such as Undertale, Fran Bow, Omori, Life is Strange, and Night in the Woods.

"HABROMANIA has an immersive art style of unplaceable nostalgia reminiscent of Over the Garden Wall or Studio Ghibli films, with lush, hand-drawn environments jam-packed with details and clues for the player to explore. The 2d visuals and animations are equal parts cozy and off-putting, like a strange dream you can't quite remember."

-HABROMANIA Kickstarter
